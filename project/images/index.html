    About Us

    “Want more information on posts and the best way to send them? Postal Pocket gives you the solutions you need.”


    Our team, Technocrats is a group of four and this application is our summer project. We are students of Institute of Informatics and Communication, Delhi University. We owe a huge thanks to NIC for joining us on this awesome journey, and we hope that you’ll continue to be a part of our story.
    POSTAL POCKET is a postal tariff calculator which offers you the best postal services at your doorstep. You can choose services for both domestic and international postal services. Once you enter your specifications, we give you the best way to send your posts anywhere and everywhere. It calculates the tariff for sending your article anywhere in few seconds!
